# 💥 WORD WAR: The Ultimate Name-Place-Animal-Thing Battle! 💥

Welcome to **Word War**, the fast-paced, comic-book style multiplayer game where your brain is your greatest weapon! 🧠⚡ 

Do you have the quick thinking to beat your friends before the clock runs out? Grab a room code, jump into the lobby, and let the chaos begin!

---

## 🎮 How to Play

**Word War** is a digital, high-octane version of the classic "Name, Place, Animal, Thing" game. Here is how you jump into the action:

1. **Host a Battle or Join a Friend:** 
   - Choose a nickname and hit **Host Match** to create a custom lobby. You can customize the total number of rounds, round time, and max players.
   - Or, click **Join Battle** and enter the Room Code given to you by the Host.

2. **Wait in the Lobby:**
   - Wait for everyone to join. While you're waiting, have some fun grabbing, throwing, and crashing the interactive floating letters around your screen! 🎈
   - When the Host is ready, they will smash the **Start Battle** button.

3. **The Round Begins!**
   - A massive **Random Letter** will appear on your screen (like "A").
   - A timer will immediately start counting down! ⏱️
   - You must quickly type a valid **Person Name**, **Place**, **Animal**, and **Thing** that all start with that exact letter.

4. **Submit Your Answers:**
   - Race to hit **Submit Answers** before time runs out! If the timer hits zero, whatever you have typed will be automatically locked in.

5. **Scoring & Challenges:**
   - After each round, everyone's answers are revealed.
   - If someone submits a fake or misspelled word, you can **Challenge** their answer to strip away their points! 🚨
   - Double points are awarded if you write a word that absolutely *nobody else* thought of! 🧠✨

## 🌟 Game Features

* **Comic Pop Aesthetic:** Enjoy a vibrant, high-contrast neobrutalist design with glowing neon lights, floating alphabets, and explosive colors.
* **Custom Lobbies:** Play with just a single best friend or invite up to 100 people into massive battle royales.
* **Smart Physics Engine:** Bored in the waiting room? Click and drag the floating background letters to throw them across the screen! They will physically bounce and crash into each other.
* **Host Superpowers:** The room host has ultimate control. They can pause the game, force an early round end, manually pick specific letters, or even kick unruly players out of the lobby! 🛡️

---

## 🤝 Rules of the War

- **No cheating!** The built-in dictionary will double-check your answers, but it's up to the players to challenge sneaky answers.
- **Speed is key.** The host sets the timer (usually 30 to 60 seconds). When the clock hits 0, there are no extensions!
- **Be unique.** The easiest way to win is to think of words your friends won't guess.
